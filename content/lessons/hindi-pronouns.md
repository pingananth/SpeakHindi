# Hindi Pronouns

Welcome to the **Hindi Pronouns** lesson! In this lesson you will learn the essential pronouns used in everyday Hindi conversation.

## Learning Goals
- Identify subject and object pronouns in Hindi.
- Use pronouns correctly in simple sentences.
- Distinguish formal vs informal pronouns.

## Content Overview

### 1. Subject Pronouns
- (main) – I
-  (tum) – you (informal)
- (aap) – you (formal)
-  (vah) – he/she/it
-  (hum) – we
-  (ve) – they

### 2. Object Pronouns (कर्म)
- मुझे (mujhe) – me
- तुम्हें (tumhein) – you (informal)
- आपको (aapko) – you (formal)
- उसे (use) – him/her/it
- हमें (humein) – us
- उन्हें (unhein) – them

### 3. Formal vs Informal Usage
- **Informal**: तुम, तुम्हें – used with friends, peers, younger people.
- **Formal**: आप, आपको – used with strangers, elders, or in professional settings.

### 4. Practice Sentences
1. **मैं किताब पढ़ रहा हूँ।** – I am reading a book.
2. **आप कैसे हैं?** – How are you? (formal)
3. **वह स्कूल जाता है।** – He goes to school.
4. **हम तुम्हें देखेंगे।** – We will see you (informal).

---

> **Tip:** Pay attention to the verb agreement when using pronouns. Hindi verbs change according to the gender and number of the subject.
